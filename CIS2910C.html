CCN: CIS2910C       Systems Development Capstone       Credit Hrs: 3.00

This capstone course is designed for the student to demonstrate his/her knowledge and skills applicable to the Computer Programming and Analysis degree core competencies and outcomes. The course is designed as a project-based experience focusing on the major phases of the System Development Life Cycle. The project requirements will be designed in conjunction with the students area of curriculum emphasis.

Pre or Corequisite(s): CIS1513C COP2171C CTS1851C
