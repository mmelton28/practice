CCN: CDA2332C Title: Systems Integration And Architecture Credit Hrs: 3.00
Description : 
This course provides the student with a detailed understanding of computer hardware and system software. The material covered in this course is intended to establish a platform of technical knowledge for systems analysis, design, configuration, procurement, and management.
 
Pre or Corequisite(s): CIS1000C COP1210C
